PWA ICONS INSTRUCTIONS:

You need to create 2 icon files for PWA:

1. icon-192.png (192x192 pixels)
2. icon-512.png (512x512 pixels)

How to create icons:

Option 1: Use online tools
- Go to https://favicon.io/ or https://realfavicongenerator.net/
- Upload your logo or create a simple design
- Download the icons and rename them to icon-192.png and icon-512.png
- Place them in the public/ folder

Option 2: Use design tools
- Create a simple square design with your logo
- Export as PNG in both 192x192 and 512x512 sizes
- Place them in the public/ folder

For now, you can use any placeholder image or create a simple text-based icon.

The icons should have:
- Your logo or brand symbol
- Background color: #316bff (blue)
- Simple, recognizable design
